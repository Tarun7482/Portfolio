About Me

Hello! I’m Tarun Kumar a B.Tech student specializing in Computer Science with a focus on Data Science at Rungta College of Engineering and Technology, Bhilai. Originally from Madhepura, Bihar, I’ve moved to Bhilai, Chhattisgarh, to pursue my education and immerse myself in the world of technology. I completed my matriculation and intermediate studies in Madhepura, where I developed a strong interest in technology and problem-solving. This led me to choose Computer Science as my major. At Rungta College, I’ve been involved in various projects related to data analysis and machine learning, gaining hands-on experience and deepening my understanding of data science.
